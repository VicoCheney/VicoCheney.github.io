Research Topics

1）*Class-name only document classification. multi-label classification, phrase generation.

​ 仅有类别名称的文件分类。多标签分类，短语生成。

2）table column typing, tabular inference.

​ 表列键入，表列推理

3）*knowledge graph, extraction, alignment, over Wikipedia lists, entities.

​ 知识图谱, 提取, 对齐, 超过维基百科列表, 实体

4）*authorship attribution of tweets, user writing style, under paraphrasing, multi-modal, new dataset

​ 推文的作者归属 用户写作风格，转述下，多模式，新数据集

5）*keyphrase generation, absent/present keyphrases, generative language model (BART)

​ 关键字的产生, 缺席/在场的关键词，生成性语言模型（BART）

6）temporal keyphrase extraction, novel keyphrase extraction/generation

​ 时间性的关键词提取, 新颖的关键词提取/生成

7）prompt learning, zero-shot learning on document classification

​ 提示学习，对文件分类的零点学习

8）long document classification

​ 长文件分类

9）question answering, with knowledge graph, aspect category detection, answer generation

​ 问题回答，与知识图谱，方面类别检测，答案生成

10）query generation, on XPath, SPARQL, SQL, regular expression, generation by seq2seq models, GAN, from sentences

​ 查询生成，基于XPath、SPARQL、SQL、正则表达式。通过seq2seq模型、GAN、从句子生成

11）summarization, joint learning with related tasks

​ 总结，与相关任务联合学习

12）sentiment analyses, on new domains.

​ 情感分析，在新的领域。
